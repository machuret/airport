Why choose us

Airport Accident Law Is
Uniquely Complex

Airports involve federal agencies, airlines, dozens of contractors, and government entities — all with different legal deadlines and claim requirements.

01
We Know Who Is Actually Liable

At most airports, a dozen companies may share responsibility for your injury — the airline, the ground handler, the cleaning contractor, the airport authority. We identify all of them before the statute of limitations runs.

02
Federal Law Deadlines Are Shorter

TSA injuries require FTCA filings before you can sue. Montreal Convention claims expire in 2 years. Government-owned airports require Notice of Claim in 60-180 days. Missing these ends your case permanently.

03
Evidence Disappears in 72 Hours

Airport CCTV footage is often overwritten within 24-72 hours. Vehicle GPS data, cleaning logs, and maintenance records can vanish. We send preservation demand letters immediately to protect your evidence.
